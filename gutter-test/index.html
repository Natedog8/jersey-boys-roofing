Prevent Water Damage

Without the proper gutter guard installation, debris buildup or ice dams can stop water from properly flowing through your gutter system. This can cause the water to overflow onto your roof and cause leaks inside your home.

Remove Fire Hazards

A buildup of dry leaves in your gutter system puts your home at risk. A single ember from a firework, adjacent campfire, or nearby brushfire can ignite the leaves and put your home and family in grave danger.

Block Pest Infestations

The right gutter guard company can help keep your home safe from pests. Insects, mice, birds, bats, and other pests can no longer nest in your gutters. Additionally, a gutter cover installation can help block these pests from crawling into your roof structure.

Limit Structural Damage

When water overflows from your gutters, it can seep between your roof boards and cause leaks inside of your home. If the leaks go undetected and either pool in your attic or drip down the wall frames, the rot and damage can cause devastating results.
