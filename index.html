Understanding Your Roof's Warning Signs

Knowing when your roof needs attention can save thousands in repairs. Jersey Boys Roofing educates homeowners about critical warning signs: water stains on ceilings indicate active leaks, missing or curled shingles expose your home to water damage, granules in gutters signal aging shingles, daylight through roof boards means immediate repair is needed, and sagging areas indicate structural problems requiring professional assessment.

Don't ignore these warning signs. What starts as a minor issue can quickly escalate into major damage. A small leak today becomes rotted decking tomorrow, mold growth next week, and structural damage next month. Jersey Boys offers free inspections to catch problems early, potentially saving you thousands in unnecessary repairs.
